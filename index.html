Capabilities

Encryption you can't see through.

Every conversation is sealed with post-quantum cryptography. The server relays bytes it cannot decrypt — and we collect no identity to begin with.

Kyber-1024 Post-Quantum
ML-KEM-1024 key exchange, AES-256-GCM payload, Double Ratchet forward secrecy. Built to outlive quantum.
No Phone Number
Sign up with a node ID — a cryptographic identifier you control. Your number stays your number.
No Email Required
No inbox to compromise, no recovery loop to phish. Lose your device, lose the account — that's the point.
Encrypted Voice Calls
WebRTC with DTLS-SRTP. End-to-end encrypted, with a TURN fallback for restrictive networks.
Encrypted Video Calls
Face-to-face that nobody intercepts. Same crypto stack as voice — no compromise for video.
Group Messaging
Encrypted group chats with sender authentication. Every participant gets their own ratchet.
Vanish Mode
Disappearing messages with per-thread timers. They go — from both phones, and from the server too.
Stealth Tunnel
Cover traffic and adaptive privacy patterns that blur the shape of your conversations.
Decoy Mode
A second PIN reveals a separate, innocuous account. Plausible deniability, built in.
Self-Hosted Server
Don't trust ours? Run your own. The server is open and ships with a one-line deploy.
Zero Metadata
No access logs. Volatile journald. Hardened server config. Even we can't say who called whom.
Built for the Paranoid
Designed for activists, journalists, and anyone who treats privacy as a default, not a feature.
