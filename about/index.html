Privacy as the baseline.

We built Secure Chat by AnA Devyra because the conversations that matter shouldn't have to live inside a profile, a phone book, or a cloud somebody else owns. Encryption is the floor - not the feature.

Mission

Why we built this.

"

Most messengers are built around an identity binding - a phone number, an email, a Google login - that quietly attaches who you are to everything you ever say.

We started over. No phone number. No email. No store gatekeeper. Just cryptography, a node ID you control, and a server that's blind by design.

- AnA Devyra Team
What we believe

Four principles. No exceptions.

Cryptography is the floor
Post-quantum from day one. Every byte of every message gets the strongest standardized cryptography we can ship.
Identity is yours
No phone, no email, no signup form bound to a real-world name. A node ID is yours to rotate the moment you want.
The server stays blind by design
Ephemeral logs, volatile state, no analytics SDK on the side. It can't read your messages, media, or calls - and it keeps no content to hand over, only the minimum routing needed to deliver a message.
Enterprise deployments
For teams and organisations that need a dedicated relay under their own control. Hardened instance pointed at your domain, same E2E crypto stack, your operations team holds the keys. For teams →
Journey

Where we are. Where we're going.

Now · live

Public v1 is live

Post-quantum encrypted messaging, plus voice + video calls, encrypted groups, Stories, voice transcribe, and smart replies - running production from a Mumbai region with a custom api.anadevyra.com domain.

Next

Switzerland becomes primary

A Switzerland region comes online as the new primary with cross-region replication, strong Swiss privacy framework alignment, and auto-failover at the discovery layer. Every installed app migrates - no APK push needed. Mumbai stays online as a regional subserver.

Then

Global subserver network

Regional subservers near every user, anchored on the Swiss primary. Enterprise partners host dedicated regional relays for their organisations. Resilience by topology, not by any single provider.

Always

Privacy stays free

Encrypted chat, voice, video, and groups are free forever. Premium adds optional LAN P2P, mesh routing, and multi-device sync - features that offload work from servers, not gatekeepers in front of basics.
